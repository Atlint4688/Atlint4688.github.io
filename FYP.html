Drowsiness Detection System

This project is a compact drowsiness detection system using the Raspberry Pi. This system prevents the drivers from falling asleep behind the wheel ensuring they would reach their target destination safely. With the use of the Raspberry Pi's built-in Wi-fi, users are able to connect their mobile devices through VNC Viewer without the need of an additional monitor. The systems utilizes an external buzzer to emit the sounds whenever the drivers status is detected as asleep. In addition, drivers can also plug their headphones onto the Raspberry Pi's built-in headphone jack for voice alerts
